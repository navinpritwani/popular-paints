About Us

The Popular Paint and Chemicals is one of the leading business conglomerates in painting group of companies.

What began in 1984 as a humble, family run trading enterprises by Founder Mansoor Zafar & Co-Founder Shabbir Zafar has today grown into a professional company led by a highly qualified & experienced senior management team. Constant Innovation, Strategic partnerships & customer focused approach have been the cornerstone of the companies phenomenal growth.
