What we are

In the ever growing talk of Rising India, and 'India at the Global stage', we tend to forget the strengths and capabilities of Bhaarat (The majority India). That is where Popular Paints and Chemicals fulfills its responsibility in a sustainable manner, by serving the rural and semi urban dwellers of Central India, with it's exclusive and comprehensive range of Paints and allied products. Established in 1985, in the then semi hinterland of Central India, and now one of the fastest growing state capitals of India, - Raipur, Popular Paints and Chemicals has been growing consistently, expanding its footprint to various states around Central India, complementing it with continuous innovation in the product categories and product quality. Popular Paints and Chemicals now boasts of a dealer network of 1200, backed by a State of the Art manufacturing facility, led by a strong and dynamic Top Management (mix of Senior Experience and Young Enthusiasm).
